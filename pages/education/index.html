Education

Tranquil Resonance Studio not only offers lessons in guqin, guzheng, and tea culture at the studio, it is also actively involved in community outreach. Having taught at various schools and culture centers, lectured at various universities throughout the bay area, we offer special presentations, lectures, demonstrations and group classes for the community. Just like the ancient masters, we firmly believe in passing on the traditional music and culture of China to everyone interested.

Group lessons for guqin and guzheng are offered on Saturday.
Private lessons for guqin and guzheng are offered throughout the week.
Private and Group lessons for tea culture are offered throughout the week.
Please contact us for available lesson times and tuition costs.

We offer group and private lessons at these other locations in the SF Bay Area. Please contact Tranquil Resonance Studio or the locations directly for more information.
